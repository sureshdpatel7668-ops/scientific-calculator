***Scientific Calculator***



Features:

It supports basic arithmetic operations, trigonometric functions(in dgree), logarithmic functions, and exponential functions. The calculator also includes a clear button to reset the display and a delete button to remove the last character entered.

Usage:

To use the calculator, simply click on the buttons to input numbers and operations. The current input will be displayed on the screen. To perform a calculation, click the "=" button, and the result will be shown on the display. You can also use parentheses to group operations and control the order of calculations.

Note:

Ensure that you enter valid mathematical expressions to avoid errors. The calculator is designed to handle a wide range of calculations, but it may not support all advanced mathematical functions.
